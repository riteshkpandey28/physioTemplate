    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    What all things are required for a consultation with YourPhysio?

    One needs to collect the following things: a) Laptop or Smartphone b) Webcam if using desktop c) Good internet speed

    Who will conduct my Tele Physiotherapy Sessions?

    Your sessions will be conducted by a qualified & experienced physiotherapist who has undergone rigorous training for providing care virtually.

    How can I share my X-Ray / MRI reports?

    You can add our WhatsApp No. +91 960 778 8208 to your contacts and send us your reports easily at any time.

    What is the duration of an online session?

    The initial consultation where our senior physiotherapist assesses your condition takes 30-45 minutes. Thereafter, every treatment session takes 45 minutes.

    Do You have Physiotherapist near me?

    We provide virtual physiotherapy services across India in cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai etc.

    Do you have any Physiotherapy clinic near me?

    We have a virtual physiotherapy clinic which is open from 7 AM to 10 PM. You can reach out to us between these timings. We will ensure that an expert physiotherapist gets in touch with you at the click of a button.
